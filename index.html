About Us

Ecobin is a smart, eco-friendly waste bin designed to promote sustainable waste management. It is equipped with IoT-based sensors and smart technology to detect and sort e-waste into appropriate categories such as batteries, wires, or circuit boards. The system aims to reduce human error in waste segregation and encourage responsible environmental behavior.

Our Mission and Vision

At Ecobin, our mission is to provide a responsible and sustainable solution for disposing of unused or damaged electronics, ensuring they are safely recycled, repurposed, or transformed into valuable resources. We envision a world where every electronic device is given a second life, fostering a circular economy that minimizes waste and maximizes innovation. Ecobin aims to lead the global shift in e-waste management by empowering individuals and businesses to make eco-conscious choices, while inspiring industries to adopt sustainable practices that protect our planet for generations to come.
