Shipping Policy

1. Service Areas

We currently offer e-waste pickup services only in Malaysia. Contact us to confirm availability in your area.

2. Scheduling Pickups

  • Requests must be submitted 2 business days in advance
  • Choose from available time slots in our online portal
  • Minimum quantity: 2 devices/pounds for free pickup

3. Preparation Guidelines

  • Securely package devices to prevent damage
  • Remove all batteries (if applicable)
  • Wipe personal data from devices (we are not responsible for remaining data)
  • Label fragile items clearly

4. Pickup Process

  • Our certified team will collect items from [your doorstep/designated area]
  • Provide tracking confirmation via email/SMS
  • Same-day rescheduling available with 12 hours notice

5. Fees & Charges

  • Free pickup for standard residential quantities
  • Expedited service fee: RM 10

6. Cancellations

  • Cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before pickup
  • Late cancellations may incur RM 10 fee

7. Damages & Liability

  • Not responsible for damages made after 24 hours from pickup
